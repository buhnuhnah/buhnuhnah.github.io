Do or Die

Skills Used:

  • UI Design/Testing
  • Character Design
  • 3D Modeling/Texturing
  • Playtesting

September 2019 - March 2020

Do or Die is my senior Capstone project. We have 20 weeks to make a game under the guidance of our mentors from Blizzard and Amazon Games. I did the art and 3D modeling since our team had no artists, but I also played an active role in the game design. I also did all of the UI testing and aided in playtests.

UI Testing

I have never had to deal with making a game UI layout, so I really wanted to make sure I did it right through extensive testing and readjusting. We didn't have a clear idea what final objects were gonna make it in the game, so I had to make multiple game mockups with different items such as abilities, stats, and health. After we reached a semi-final decision, I made the actual UI elements and went around performing UI tests on people with different levels of experience with games. A total of 12 different layouts were tested. Two weeks before the deadline we decided last minute to add in 3 distinct player classes, 6 unique movement and attack abilities, and 6 types of pickups. Because of this it was a rush to get each icon finished and tested to make sure each ability's effect was clear.

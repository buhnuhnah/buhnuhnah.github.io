Shopping Season Deluxe 2.0:
Attack of the Seasoning

Skills Used:

  • 2D Animation
  • Character Design
  • Digital Art
Download

January - March 2019

This was a game that me and 4 others made in 10 weeks for a class. The prompt word was "seasoning", so we came up with a timed minigame where the player has to try to collect as many spices in a busy mall as they can. Each level is procedurally generated, so replaying it is always fresh. I did all of the art assets, making sure that they worked well with the level generator.

Art Assets

Process

Animating

This was my first time doing art digitally, so I used the free program Krita to experiment with. This was a huge learning experience as I learned about animation. The three animations (run, jump, grab) took about a week and a half to do since I had no base knowledge, but I had a fun time doing it!

Character Design

Since our prompt was "seasoning", I wanted to find a way to incorporate spices into the character design. We also needed our character to stand out since we knew our game was going to have a lot of visual elements to give the feeling of a busy shopping mall.

Hi, I'm Ana!

I'm a game designer who creates games to develop my interests in things like animation, AI, and character creation. No matter what the medium, I love creating colorful, happy things that make people smile! I'm a big fan of telling stories, and I think games are the perfect medium to do so. We only get 24 hours in a day, so if players decide to invest their valuable time playing a game, they should come out of it feeling GOOD.

If I'm not playing or making video games, you can find me throwing myself head first into whatever new interest has caught my eye.

Fix it Faucet

Skills Used:

  • 2D Animation
  • Vector Art
  • Character Design

January 31, 2020 - February 2, 2020

This game was made in 48 hours for Global Game Jam 2020 with 2 friends. The prompt was "repair", and since we started late we decided to make a simple puzzle game that randomly generates puzzles for endless gameplay. I did all of the art once again, and together the three of us came up with Fix it Faucet!

Process

Character Design

Since there wasn't very much time to experiment with different characters, I gave myself 30 minutes to come up with a character that would be quirky and interesting while not distracting players from the puzzle. I wanted her to be like a cute mascot that almost cheers on players, and I also wanted her to be a plumber to enhance the pipes theme. Originally we wanted to incorporate a plunger into her design as a gag, but when the faucet pigtails were proposed again, we unanimously agreed they were cuter. The character's name is Pipp.

Vector Art

I had never done much work in vector art before, but when it was time to make an icon for the game, I decided to give it a shot. I used the Vectornator app on the ipad alongside Procreate to sketch and create the finished icon of Pipp.

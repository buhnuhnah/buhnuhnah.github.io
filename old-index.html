Hi, I'm Ana!

I'm a game designer who creates games to develop my interests in things like animation, AI, and character creation. No matter what the medium, I love creating colorful, happy things that make people smile! I believe that time is precious, so if players decide to invest their valuable time playing a game, they should come out of it feeling GOOD.

If I'm not playing or making video games, you can find me throwing myself headfirst into whatever new interest has caught my eye.
